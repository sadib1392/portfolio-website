Synergy Prep Website Concept

The Company

Synergy Prep is a private tutoring company founded in 2015 by two graduates of NYIT in New York City. The founders, Fahim Rofique, and Fatin Fuad Nabil were just students in university when they decided to open up their first tutoring center.

Their goal was to create a space where kids who are at a disadvantage because of their economic background would be able to get affordable tutoring and be as successful as their peers who did not face the same financial burdens.

Project Goals

The goal of this project was to make sure that the Synergy Prep brand was associated with quality tutoring but affordable pricing.

Another goal was to show that Synergy Prep is a fun tutoring center and it was founded by youthful but intelligent and professional individuals.
